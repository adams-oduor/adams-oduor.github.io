About Me

Building equitable
talent systems

I'm a Technology Talent Acquisition Lead with 4+ years of experience. Currently at M-KOPA — a pan-African fintech operating across Kenya, Uganda, Ghana, Nigeria, South Africa, and the UK. I lead full-cycle recruitment for Technology, Product, Credit, and E-Mobility functions while simultaneously serving as Ashby ATS Systems Administrator.

My work sits at the intersection of people strategy and systems thinking. I've built automation workflows, AI-augmented screening frameworks, and recruitment tooling that make hiring faster, fairer, and more scalable — not just for M-KOPA, but as open models for the wider African tech ecosystem.

Outside of work, I'm a skater, poet, and artist. I believe the same creative instincts that fuel great art make for more human, more memorable recruitment experiences.
